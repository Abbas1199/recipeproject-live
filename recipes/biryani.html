Chicken Biryani

it is very tasty and yummy especially if "cooked". There are a lot of variation in the biryani but people who eat vegetable biryani are the absolute non humans .

Chicken Biryani is a delicious savory rice dish that is loaded with spicy marinated chicken, caramelized onions, and flavorful saffron rice. For my Biryani, I simplify the order of operations, while retaining the traditional layered approach to assembling it.

    Ingridients

  • Tender chicken 1KG
  • Basmati Rice 1KG
  • Fresh curd 1/2 Ltr
  • Garam masala 100mgs
  • Red chilli Powder 100 mgs
  • Tomatoes, Onions
  • salt and pepper according to taste

    Steps to cook Biryani
  1. Make a mixture of red chilli powder, curd, garam masala, and salt
  2. Apply it well on the chicken and set it aside for an hour
  3. Boil the rice and cook it half
  4. Fry the onions and till brown then add cut Tomatoes, then add the half cooked rice.
  5. mix the chicken that was kept aside
  6. Cook for another half an hour and it will be ready
  7. Now the biryani will be reasy to eat! Serve Hot
